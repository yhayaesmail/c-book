Use Case: "Synchronized Discussion Cycles"

Change History: 2026-04-08 – Version 1.0 – Initial version
Use Case NumberUC-SYNC-DISC-01
Brief DescriptionA state machine that unlocks discussion threads only after the group reaches a specific reading milestone. Prevents spoilers and keeps everyone on the same pace.
ActorsRegistered User (club member)
TriggersMember marks progress (e.g., finishes chapter 5).
Pre-ConditionsClub has active reading schedule with milestones (e.g., chapters 1-5 by Monday).
Post-ConditionsNew discussion thread unlocked for that milestone. Members can view and post.

Standard Procedure

User ActionSystem Response
1. (Member) Marks a chapter as read.Updates individual progress. Checks if milestone reached.
2. –If milestone reached (e.g., 50% of members completed chapter 5), unlocks corresponding discussion thread.
3. –Sends notification to all members: "Chapter 5 discussion now open".
4. (Member) Posts in thread.Stores post. Displays only to members who have also unlocked that milestone.
5. –Prevents members who haven't reached the milestone from viewing the thread (spoiler protection).
Validation & Business Rules
  • Milestone can be based on individual progress or group progress (e.g., 70% of members).
  • Owner can manually override lock/unlock.
  • Threads are automatically locked after the next milestone is reached.
  • Late‑joining members receive backfilled unlocked threads.
Alternative Flows
  • Member skips ahead – system warns but allows with confirmation.
  • Owner removes milestone – thread remains open.
  • Milestone never reached – system sends reminder to members.
